I’m going back to focusing my energy on one topic at a time.

The reading list has served its purpose for this season in my life. It was reborn out of necessity, I didn’t have the interest/energy to do deep dives into topics anymore, and had very scattered bits of time to write things down. This format suited that constraint well

It was my way to do the bare minimum, a way to keep engaged in the practice of writing things down.
