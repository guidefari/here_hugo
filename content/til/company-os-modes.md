Some more analogies to expand on the quadrants:

# Intentional + Implicit
- Apprenticeship: "We pair new people with veterans. You learn by osmosis."
- "If you don't get it after a few months, you're probably not a culture fit."
- "We talk through decisions, mostly in real time"

In my opinion, this mode works best with smaller, mature teams.

# Inentional + Explicit
- Transparent by default: "We document decisions publicly so anyone can understand the why."
- Playbooks as living documents. Encouraged for everyone to contribute to them

# Afterthought + Implicit
- "Bella is the only person who knows the how/why - go to her"
